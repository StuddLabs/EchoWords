# EchoWords 🔊

EchoWords is a lightweight web application designed to help English learners improve their **listening comprehension** through interactive audio exercises.

The platform presents dialogues where some words are intentionally removed from the transcript. Learners must carefully listen to the audio and fill in the missing words based on what they hear.

This approach helps students actively train their ears and develop stronger listening skills in English.

---

## 📚 Project Background

EchoWords was originally created in **2023** during English classes at a software consulting company.

Because many of the company’s clients were international, developers participated in regular English training sessions. During these lessons, it became clear that listening comprehension was one of the most challenging skills for the team.

To support both the teacher and the students, this project was developed as a simple tool to practice listening through **interactive gap-fill exercises based on audio dialogues**.

---

## 🎯 Learning Objective

EchoWords focuses on strengthening one of the most difficult aspects of language learning:

**Listening comprehension.**

By using the application, learners can:

* Improve their ability to understand spoken English
* Recognize words and expressions in natural conversations
* Reinforce vocabulary through contextual listening
* Practice active listening instead of passive audio consumption
* Develop better confidence when interacting in English

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. The learner plays an audio dialogue.
2. A transcript appears with **missing words**.
3. The learner types the words they believe were spoken.
4. The system checks the answers and provides feedback.

This exercise format is commonly used in language learning environments and helps simulate real listening challenges.

## 💡 Why Listening Practice Matters

Listening is often the most difficult skill for English learners because real spoken language includes:

* Fast speech
* Connected words
* Different accents
* Natural rhythm and intonation

EchoWords helps learners become more comfortable with real spoken English by encouraging **focused listening practice**.
